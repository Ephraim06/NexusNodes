Location

Sunset Beach, Cape Town

ELEGANCE UNVEILED

Sunset Beach, an upscale estate just north of Cape Town, offers property owners breathtaking views of Table Mountain and Robben Island. Known for its pristine white beach, popular among windsurfers and kitesurfers, this exclusive area boasts not only magnificent scenery but also a reputation for exciting activities and impressive waves.

Despite its tranquility and luxury, Sunset Beach is in close proximity to Cape Town's attractions, including the expansive Canal Walk mall. The estate offers a range of dining options, pubs, and convenient access to excellent healthcare and top-tier government and private schools, enhancing the overall appeal of the area.

Why Albus Waveside Villa?

SECURE LIVING IN SUNSET BEACH

Sunset Beach is highly sought after by international investors due to its popularity in the real estate market. The area is renowned for its exceptional waves, particularly during the South-East winds in the summer, making it a hub for skilled surfers, kitesurfers, kiteboarders, and boardsailors.

Despite its tranquil and luxurious ambiance, Sunset Beach is conveniently close to Cape Town's attractions, including the expansive Canal Walk mall and entertainment complex. The vicinity boasts a diverse selection of restaurants and pubs, complemented by easy access to a first-rate hospital and top-tier government and private schools, contributing to the area's outstanding infrastructure.
